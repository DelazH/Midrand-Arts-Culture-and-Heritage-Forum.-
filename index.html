Our Action Areas

What We Deliver to the Community

Our structured programs address critical developmental, training, and social challenges faced by youth and emerging creatives in Gauteng.

🎨

Community Arts Development

Opening up cultural access. Establishing workshops and training avenues for visual, performing, and musical arts in local neighborhoods.

🚀

Support for Emerging Artists

Providing incubation, professional mentorship, showcase opportunities, and connection to creative industries for career growth.

🌍

Cultural & Heritage Preservation

Documenting, celebrating, and sustaining indigenous knowledge systems, local heritage landmarks, and traditional practices.

🎓

Skills & Youth Empowerment

Running computer literacy, creative writing, digital media production, and arts business management courses for out-of-school youth.

🎉

Events & Performances

Hosting neighborhood festivals, exhibitions, poetry slams, and theatrical shows that bring people together and boost local economic activity.

🏢

NPO & Org Development

Offering guidance, training, and operational support to other nascent grassroots culture clubs on regulatory compliance and funding.

Help Us Sustain Our Community Programs

2. Workshop Materials & Equipment (Art/Sound) 25%

4. Admin & Municipal Facility Overhead upkeep 15%
